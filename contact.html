FAQ

how to cancel booking?

Free cancellation: You can cancel within a set timeframe (e.g., 24 hours before) without penalty. Partial refund: You receive a partial refund based on the cancellation period (e.g., 50% if cancelled 7 days before). Non-refundable: No refunds are offered for cancellations. Online: You can go on cancellation option within your account dashboard. Email: If online cancellation isn't available, contact the platform or property owner directly via email with your booking details and cancellation request.

when will I get the possession?

This is the legal transfer of ownership. The closing date is typically set 30-60 days after the offer is accepted, depending on the complexity of the transaction and the workload of involved parties (lawyers, lenders, etc.)

where can I pay the rent?

Accepted payment methods: Bank transfers and Mobile Money Credit/Debit cards: Major credit and debit cards are accepted with secure processing.

how to contact with the buyers?

Reach out to buyers through various channels such as email, phone calls, social media platforms, and messaging apps. By diversifying your communication channels, you increase the likelihood of reaching buyers where they are most active.

why my listing not showing up?

Complete all required fields: Double-check that you've filled in all mandatory fields, including descriptions, photos, property details, and pricing. Incomplete listings might not be displayed.

how to promote my listing?

High-quality photos & videos: Professional photos and engaging virtual tours can make all the difference. Showcase your property in its best light with clear, well-lit images and immersive video experiences.
